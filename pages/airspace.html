Airspace

Understanding airspace allows pilots to safely operate around airports, controlled areas, and other aircraft.

Airspace Classes

Class A

High altitude controlled airspace used by airline and instrument pilots.

Class B

Busy airport airspace requiring specific clearance before entering.

Class C

Airspace around medium-sized airports requiring communication with ATC.

Class D

Controlled airspace around airports with operating control towers.

Class E

Controlled airspace that supports instrument operations.

Class G

Uncontrolled airspace where pilots are responsible for separation.
