Cessna 172

The Cessna 172 Skyhawk is one of the most popular training aircraft in the world. It is widely used for private pilot training because of its stability, reliability, and forgiving flight characteristics.

Aircraft Specifications

  • Engine: Lycoming IO-360 (varies by model)
  • Seats: 4
  • Cruise Speed: Approximately 120 knots
  • Range: Approximately 600 nautical miles
  • Category: Single-engine land aircraft

Training Notes

The Cessna 172 helps student pilots learn essential skills including takeoffs, landings, traffic patterns, navigation, and emergency procedures.

Pilot Tips

  • Maintain proper airspeed during all phases of flight.
  • Practice smooth control inputs.
  • Always use proper checklist procedures.
  • Develop good weather and risk management habits.
