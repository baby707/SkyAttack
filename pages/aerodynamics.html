Aerodynamics

Understanding how an aircraft flies is one of the most important foundations for every pilot.

The Four Forces of Flight

Lift

The upward force created by the wings that allows an aircraft to climb and stay airborne.

Weight

The force of gravity pulling the aircraft toward the Earth.

Thrust

The forward force produced by the engine and propeller.

Drag

The resistance that slows the aircraft as it moves through the air.
