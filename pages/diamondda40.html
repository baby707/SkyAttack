Diamond DA40

The Diamond DA40 is a modern, efficient, single-engine training aircraft known for its composite construction, advanced avionics, and excellent safety features. It is commonly used by flight schools around the world.

Aircraft Specifications

  • Engine: Lycoming IO-360 or Austro AE300 (depending on model)
  • Seats: 4
  • Cruise Speed: Approximately 120-130 knots
  • Range: Approximately 700 nautical miles
  • Category: Single-engine land aircraft

Training Notes

The DA40 introduces student pilots to modern cockpit technology, including glass displays and advanced aircraft systems. It helps pilots develop strong instrument and automation awareness.

Pilot Tips

  • Become comfortable with glass cockpit displays and avionics.
  • Maintain proper energy management during approaches.
  • Understand aircraft systems before relying on automation.
  • Continue practicing strong checklist habits.
